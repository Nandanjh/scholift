Common questions about our upcoming school management system for Bihar schools.

When will SchoLift be available for schools in Bihar?

SchoLift is currently under development specifically for Bihar schools. We're working diligently to bring this platform to you soon. Contact us to receive updates on our launch timeline.

Do schools need special technical infrastructure to use SchoLift?

No, SchoLift is designed to work with minimal technical requirements. A basic computer or tablet with internet access is sufficient. The system is specifically built for schools without dedicated IT staff.

How will parents receive updates without downloading an app?

Parents will receive updates via WhatsApp and SMS messages directly to their phones. There's no need for parents to download or learn a new app, making the system accessible to all parents regardless of their technical proficiency.

Will training be provided to school staff?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training to all school staff. The system is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, with a focus on simplicity for non-technical users. Our team will ensure your staff is comfortable using all features.

How can we migrate our existing school records to SchoLift?

SchoLift includes a seamless data migration system. We'll help you transfer all your existing student records, whether they're in digital format or paper-based. The process is designed to be simple and require minimal effort from your staff.
